Pangaea Kids — Privacy Policy

App: Pangaea Kids (Android package com.tolga.pangaeakids)
Developer: Tolga Şencan  ·  Contact: rockoroma@gmail.com
Effective date: 7 July 2026

Pangaea Kids is a dinosaur puzzle game designed for children. We built it to comply with the Google Play Families Policy, the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), and the GDPR's protections for children. The short version: we do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

1. Information we collect: none

  • No account or registration exists in the app.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, photos, contacts, location, or any other personal information.
  • The app contains no chat, social features, or free-text input.
  • Game progress (which puzzles are completed) is saved only on the device and is deleted when the app is uninstalled. It is never uploaded anywhere.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Some dinosaurs can be played once by watching a short, clearly labelled reward video. These ads are shown by Google AdMob and are always started by a button press — never automatically. Because Pangaea Kids is a children's app, we configure AdMob in child-directed mode:

  • Ads are requested with Google's tag for child-directed treatment (COPPA-compliant), and the maximum ad content rating is set to G (suitable for all audiences).
  • Ads are contextual only — never personalized. Behavioural advertising is disabled for this app.
  • The app does not use, and removes the Android permission for, the device's Advertising ID.
  • Google may process limited technical data (such as IP address and device type) to serve and secure ads. See Google's Privacy Policy and how Google uses information from apps that use AdMob.

3. Purchases (Google Play)

The app offers a single one-time purchase ("Unlock all dinos") handled entirely by Google Play Billing. The purchase screen sits behind a parental gate intended for adults. We never see or store payment details — Google processes the payment and only tells the app that the unlock is owned. See Google's Privacy Policy for how Google handles payment data.

4. Children's privacy

Pangaea Kids is directed at children and treats every user as a child: no personal data is collected from anyone, ads are child-directed and contextual only, and there are no links out of the app other than the store pages described above. Since we collect nothing, there is nothing for us to delete; removing the app removes all locally saved progress.

5. Changes to this policy

If the app's data practices ever change, we will update this page and the effective date above before the change ships.

6. Contact

Questions or concerns — parents included — are welcome at rockoroma@gmail.com.
